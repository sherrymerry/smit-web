Food Distribution Programs

Saylani operates large-scale soup kitchens and food distribution points across Pakistan, providing free meals to the needy on a daily basis. These initiatives serve thousands of meals every day, ensuring that no one goes hungry.

Skill Development and Vocational Training:

Saylani offers vocational training programs aimed at equipping individuals with skills that can help them earn a livelihood and become financially independent. These programs cover a wide range of trades and professions, including tailoring, carpentry, plumbing, and IT skills, enabling participants to acquire practical skills and enhance their employability.
